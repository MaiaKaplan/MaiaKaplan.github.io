Aren't you a Biologist?

I have an undergraduate degree in biology, sure, and recently a Masters in biology as well, woop! Though bioinformatics has been the focus of my research for most of those studies ranging from phylogenetic analyses, to whole genome sequencing, to comparative studies that use the variation between species to guide biological experiments.

Research Interests

Broadly, I am interested in biological problems that benefit from computational tools. Based on my background, I tend towards genetics and evoltuion, and how sequencing data can be used to provide supplementary information (such as ancestral relatedness, conserved sequences that may so for functional reasons, sequencings to represent species diversity).
